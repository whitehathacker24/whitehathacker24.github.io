How To Play: 1v1.LOL

Controls LMB to shoot/build

WASD to move

Space bar to jump

Shift to crouch

Z, X, C, V, or Y to switch building platforms

F, 1, or 2 to switch weapons

R to rotate stair/reload

G to make door on platforms

E to open doors


About 1v1.LOL

It's a combination of a construction simulator and 3rd person shooter game. The game is simply built so you can explore the great features that the game development team has built.

Inspired by Fortnite, the game is compact, and can be played completely in any browser.

Outstanding Features

1v1 lol gives you 4 game modes, and each game mode has 4 different options for you to experience. There is a single mode for you to practice and improve your skills, and 3 team modes for you to compete against other players.

Free Build

This mode is completely focused so you can promote your construction skills, unlimited space, and endless construction resources, enough for your creativity to be satisfied.

Aim Trainer

But the name is a place for you to practice skills with in-game items. If you don't have quick hands and great reflexes, this is a good environment

Zombies

You will need full skill and use items to fight off thousands of zombies that attack you. Just you and the world is filled with zombies. Be prepared for the tough challenge

Pipe Run

It is a mode that helps you practice reflexes and the ability to control your character. You need to move through the holes as fast as you can. Once you have achieved achievements in this mode, you will definitely be an unpredictable opponent in all arenas

Competition mode in 1v1 lol

After completing the challenges to practice skills in the game. You will explore the competitive modes of the game. 1v1 lol gives you 1v1 2v2 or 3v3 challenge. These challenges all require you to have good skills and good coordination with your teammates. Each challenge can compete in a specific arena or a certain terrain. You'll need to get used to it all before finding the optimal terrain for you.
